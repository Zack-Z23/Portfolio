User Guide on how to send images on Skype:



      1. Open the Skype app

      2. Go to the conversation you want to send the image.


      3. Towards the right of the app, you should see 4 different icons outside the message bar.


      4. Click on the first icon towards the left that is outside the message bar.


      5. It will open a minimized version of the File Explorer


      6. Go to Pictures(Or wherever your images might be stored).


      7. Click on the image you want to send.


      8. Click Open after you have selected the image.


      9. Press enter or click on the arrow with a background of blue to send your image.
